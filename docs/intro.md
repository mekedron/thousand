# Thousand

**Thousand** (Russian: *Тысяча*; Polish: *Tysiąc*; Ukrainian: *Тисяча*) is a
classic point-trick card game from the **Marriage family**, popular across
Eastern and Central Europe. The objective is exactly what the name suggests:
**be the first player to reach 1000 points**.

The game is famous for two ingredients that combine luck with sharp tactical
play:

1. **Auction bidding** — players bid points to win the right to choose trump.
2. **Marriages** — declaring a King + Queen of the same suit during play
   instantly switches the trump suit and awards a large bonus.

This documentation describes the **standard 3-player Russian rules** as the
canonical reference, then catalogues the most common regional and house-rule
variations.

## Who this guide is for

- Newcomers who want to learn the game from scratch.
- Returning players who need a quick reference.
- Anyone curious about the **regional differences** between the Russian,
  Polish, Ukrainian and other variants.

## A note on rules

Thousand has **no single international authority**. Two tables in the same
city will often disagree on details — the value of the diamond marriage, the
penalty for a failed barrel, whether a player may pass after seeing the
talon. Wherever the rules differ meaningfully we point this out, and the
[Variations](./variations/) section is a deliberate, opinionated catalogue of
the most common alternatives. **Always agree the house rules before the first
deal.**
