# Trump & Marriages

Thousand has a unique trump mechanism: **trump is not chosen up front**.
There is **no trump at all** until a player declares a **marriage**. The
trump suit then changes every time a different marriage is declared.

## What is a marriage?

A **marriage** is the **King + Queen of the same suit** held in one player's
hand.

- **♥ Hearts marriage** — 100 points
- **♦ Diamonds marriage** — 80 points
- **♣ Clubs marriage** — 60 points
- **♠ Spades marriage** — 40 points

## How to declare a marriage

A marriage is declared by **leading either the King or the Queen of that
suit at the start of a trick**, and announcing the marriage.

1. The player must be **on lead** (winning the previous trick).
2. They lead the K or Q of the suit.
3. They announce: *"Marriage in hearts"* (or whatever the suit is).
4. The marriage value is **immediately added** to that player's score for
   the deal.
5. **The trump suit becomes the suit of the marriage**, effective from the
   *next* trick onward.

The marriage card itself is then played as a normal lead in the current
trick — it is not a free declaration.

:::warning[The lead must be from the K or Q]
Holding K + Q is not enough. The marriage is **only** scored when one of
the two cards is led from the player's hand on the lead. Cards captured in
tricks do not count.
:::

## Multiple marriages

A player may hold and declare **several marriages** in a single deal. Each
new declaration **replaces** the trump suit. A player who holds, say, the
♦K-Q and ♣K-Q can declare diamonds (80 + trump becomes ♦), then later
declare clubs (60 + trump becomes ♣).

## No trump until a marriage

Until the first marriage of the deal is declared, **there is no trump
suit**. Tricks are won by the highest card of the led suit; a player who
cannot follow suit simply discards a low card (it cannot win).

If **no marriage is ever declared** during a deal, the entire hand is
played without trump.

## Marriage in the talon

If the declarer picks up a King or Queen from the talon that completes a
marriage, that marriage is **legal** and may be declared in the usual way
(by leading the K or Q). Many strong bids depend exactly on this scenario.

## Following suit and trumping

Once trump exists, the standard trick-taking rules apply with two
**Thousand-specific** quirks documented next: see
[Trick-taking](./trick-taking).
